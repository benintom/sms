Welcome to Triune Academy of Music



Our Lady of Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, Nettoor, is the first in Kerala to offer novenas to Mother Mary in Malayalam. Every Saturday, thousands flock here to plea their petitions or to offer thanks for the favours received. Blessed Virgin Mary, through the most sacred heart of Jesus comes to the aid of the miserable with speedy and necessary help, especially in the most desperate cases.

History of Triune

Our Lady of Immaculate Heart of Mary Church belonging to the Archdiocese of Verapoly is located in the midst of Ernakulam city, (near Vyttila) and has completed 70 years of its fruitful service to the society. Nettoor area was under the Venduruthy parish and irrespective of Latin – Syrian rites, people used to attend the church. In 1970, IHM Church, Nettoor, became an independent parish and Rev. Fr. Joseph Thattarasserry was appointed as the first Parish Priest. And thereafter, it did not take long to become a pilgrimage centre of the faithful.
